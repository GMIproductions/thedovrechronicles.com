Burning Questions?

What's this 'who dunnit' about?

It's a puzzle! Figure out who's behind the mountain's mischief. Think you're smart enough? Prove it.

Are the characters real?

Of course they're real! They live in the pages of the book, in our imaginations, in the ancient lore of the land! They're characters - yes, and they feel pretty darn alive. Don't get too attached.

What's with the submarine?

The best stories include at least one submarine. Period.

Can I buy the book in German or Norwegian?

Ja, Ja, and Ja! The book is translated and ready for you in English, German, and Norwegian. No excuses for not reading it. Unless you don't speak English, German or Norwegian of course - in which case you're not reading this anyways...

Who is the Mountain King?

Well, he's the Mountain King - ruler of all Dovre trolls, you know? If you need more details, you'll just have to read the book to find out. No more beans spilt by us!
